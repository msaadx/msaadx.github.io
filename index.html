About

Engineering AI with the rigour of research and the discipline of production.

I'm a Machine Learning Engineer based in Islamabad, Pakistan, and a Computer Science graduate of FAST (NUCES). My work lives at the intersection of applied AI engineering and hands-on research — I love taking an idea from a paper or a blank repository all the way to systems that real businesses depend on.

At Levitate Data I work in the core AI engineering team behind Thalamus, an enterprise-grade agentic RAG platform, and I led the end-to-end architecture of Aletheia, a multimodal analysis engine that serves as the AI brain behind Cognition Legal. Alongside this, I dig into deep-learning research in medical imaging — rebuilding novel architectures from scratch, training them, and improving on them until they rival far heavier models.

What really drives me is curiosity. I love learning how the world works — and I find it astonishing that, by decoding the fundamental forces of nature, we eventually understood enough to build artificial intelligence itself, drawing inspiration from the architecture of the human brain. Chasing that thread, from first principles to working systems, is what keeps me building every day.

Research & Development

Original deep-learning research, benchmarked hard.

A body of work on parameter-efficient architectures for multi-modal medical imaging — implementing, improving and inventing methods that match heavy baselines at a fraction of the cost.

01 Novel architecture

Spectral-KAN

Kolmogorov–Arnold Networks × Fourier Neural Operators

A novel architecture using custom ChebyshevConv2D layers (learnable degree-3 polynomials instead of linear weights) and a Global Spectral Gating mechanism built on 2D FFTs with learnable complex frequency filters — for global spatial routing across X-ray, MRI and endoscopy.

  • ~413Kparameters
  • 93.43%test accuracy
  • 0.94weighted F1
02

Dynamic Ensemble Knowledge Distillation

Multi-modal medical classification

A 1.9M-parameter MobileNetV2 student with Coordinate Attention, distilled from an EfficientNetV2B0 + ResNet50V2 ensemble using entropy-based dynamic weighting and MixUp.

  • 1.9Mparams
  • 97.56%accuracy
  • >0.99ROC-AUC
03

KDLight

Lightweight knowledge distillation · 14 conditions

A compressed CNN distilled from a MobileNetV1 teacher via composite hard + temperature-scaled KL-divergence loss, profiled rigorously for real-world deployment.

  • ~90%less memory
  • 4×faster inference
  • 86.7%accuracy
04

U-Net + Few-Shot TB Classification

Two-stage segmentation & meta-learning

A 7.7M-parameter U-Net for lung-field segmentation feeding a Prototypical Network (MobileNetV2) for 2-way, 5-shot tuberculosis classification from scarce data.

  • 0.96Dice score
  • 98%val accuracy
  • 5-shotmeta-learning
05

Evolutionary MobileNet-ConvLSTM2D

Hybrid net · evolutionary kernel search

A hybrid MobileNet + Inception + ConvLSTM2D classifier across 9 medical conditions, with a novel "Chain Foraging Cyclone Aging" evolutionary algorithm for dynamic kernel-size optimization.

  • 94%test accuracy
  • 9conditions
  • 7K+images
